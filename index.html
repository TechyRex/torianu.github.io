FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

To contact us, you can reach out to our dedicated Client Service through TORÍ ÀÁNÚ's official website. Visit the "Contact Us" section, where you'll find email addresses and phone numbers to connect with us.

We strive to respond to all inquiries promptly, but response times may vary depending on the volume of messages we receive.

In-Store Appointments offer a personalized experience within our physical boutiques. Book your appointment on the website — you will receive a confirmation via email or phone. Once you arrive at your chosen store, you can enjoy priority access without having to wait in line. Our Client Advisor will assist you throughout your visit, offering advice on everything from styling and gifting to aftercare.

Our Client Service team is available to handle any assistance or repair requests for TORÍ ÀÁNÚ products. Our Client Advisors will recommend the best solutions based on an initial assessment of the item and will provide updates throughout the process. From iconic accessories like bags and wallets to sunglasses, shoes, and valuable pieces like jewellery and watches, we offer support for every product. Our after-sales service is also available at our boutiques and through authorized retailers.
